About Me

Crafting Beauty,
One Strand at a Time

Hi, I'm Emily — a passionate hair artist based in Hoboken, New Jersey. With over 15 years of experience, I've dedicated my career to making every client feel like the most beautiful version of themselves.

After training at the prestigious Bumble and bumble University in NYC and working alongside some of the industry's finest, I opened my own studio to create a personal, luxury experience. My specialties include balayage & ombre, color corrections, precision cutting, and bridal styling.

Every head of hair tells a story — and I'm here to make yours a beautiful one. Whether you're looking for a subtle refresh or a bold transformation, I'll work with you to create a look that's uniquely you.
